Sustainable Development Goal Quality Education

"Quality Education" is one of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) outlined in the United Nations' 2030 Agenda for Sustainable Development. It is represented by Goal 4 and focuses on ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education and promoting lifelong learning opportunities for all.

Key aspects of Goal 4, "Quality Education," include:

  • Access to Education
  • Equity and Inclusion
  • Quality Learning
  • Lifelong Learning
  • Teacher Training
  • Gender Equality
  • Access to Technical and Vocational Education
  • Global Citizenship Education
  • Education Infrastructure
  • Data Collection and Monitoring

Strategies to Advance Quality Education

  • Policy Reforms
  • Teacher Training and Support
  • Inclusive Education
  • Curriculum Development
  • Community Engagement
  • Technology Integration
  • International Cooperation
